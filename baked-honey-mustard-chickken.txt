Ingredients:
 - 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
 - 1/2 cup prepared mustard
 - 1 teaspoon dried basil
 - 1 teaspoon paprika
 - 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
 - 1/2 cup honey
 - salt and peper to taste

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
 2. Sprinkle chicken breasts with salt and pepper to taste, and place in a
    lightly greased 9x13 inch backing dish.
 3. In a small bowl, combine the honey, mustard, basil, paprika, and parsley. 
    Mix well.  Pour 1/2 of this mixture over the chicken, and brush to cover.
 4. Bake in the preheated over for 30 minutes.
 5. Turn chicken pieces over and brush with the remaining 1/2 of the honey
    mustard mixture.
 5. Bake for an additional 10 to 15 minutes, or until the chicken is no longer
    pink and juices run clear.
 6. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
